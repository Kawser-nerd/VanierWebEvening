Section I: Course Plan

All the classes and exams will take place in online. Students are required to access their webcam during the exam time and at the time of any assessments. Teacher will record the class and share that with the students after the class.

Section II: Require Textbook/Materials

No specific book is recommended. The student can use any related textbook* for learning the materials.

All the class recordings, required documentation1 and tutorials will be provided to students throughout the semester. The instructor is open to use and refer any online tutorials whenever required
